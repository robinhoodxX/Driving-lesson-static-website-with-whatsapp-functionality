About Us

At Green Pass, we believe learning to drive should be a safe, confident, and enjoyable experience. Our mission is to equip everyone desirous with the skills, knowledge, and road awareness needed to become a responsible and capable driver for life. With a team of highly qualified and patient instructors, we offer personalized lessons tailored to your pace, whether you’re a complete beginner, need a refresher course, or want to master advanced driving techniques. We focus not only on passing the driving test but also on building real-world confidence behind the wheel. Our modern, well-maintained training vehicles are equipped with dual controls to ensure maximum safety during lessons. We provide flexible scheduling, transparent pricing, and a friendly, supportive learning environment. Whether your goal is to get your first license or improve your driving skills, Green L is here to guide you every step on the way — helping you drive with skill, safety, and confidence.

Business terms and conditions

Lessons can be booked online or over the phone.
• Punctuality: The instructor will wait for a maximum of 15 minutes. If the student does not arrive, the lesson will be treated as cancelled without notice and charged accordingly.
• Instructor Car Use: The vehicle is covered by insurance for use during lessons and the driving test only. It is not insured for the student’s independent practice outside of scheduled lesson times.
• Driving Test Bookings: The instructor may refuse to provide the car for a driving test if they believe the student is not adequately prepared, in order to ensure safety. The fee for the use of the car will be charged on the day of the test which is separate from the DVSA test fee.
• Refunds: Refunds for pre-paid lesson blocks are given at the discretion of management and are calculated at the standard single lesson rate for any lessons already taken
• Cancellation policy: We kindly ask for at least 48 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your lessons. This allows us to offer your slot to another student. Lessons cancelled with less than 48 hours notice will be charged in full.
